About Kadrama

Village Life

Kadrama is a peaceful rural community located in Olpad Taluka of Surat district. Agriculture, culture and strong community bonds define the lifestyle of the villagers.

Agriculture

Most families depend on farming and agriculture related activities. The fertile land around the village supports crops and farming livelihoods.

Culture

The village celebrates traditional Gujarati festivals with enthusiasm including Navratri, Uttarayan and Holi.

Village Development

Panchayat

The Gram Panchayat plays an important role in development, welfare programs and infrastructure improvements.

Infrastructure

Road connectivity, electricity and local facilities continue to improve through government schemes and community participation.

Education

Local schools support children's education and literacy growth within the village.

Sports in Kadrama

Jai Verai Maa Cricket Club

One of the most active cricket clubs in the village organizing tournaments and local matches.

Kadrama Cricket Club

A popular club representing village pride in cricket competitions.

Cricket Culture

Cricket is one of the most loved sports in the village. Local tournaments bring together players and spectators from nearby villages.
